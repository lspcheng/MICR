Informed Consent

Purpose of the study

The purpose of this study is to examine listener judgments about the naturalness of speech.

Description

In this experiment, you will listen to sentences spoken by individuals who are from either the U.S. or Canada. You will be asked to rate how natural their speech sounds on a scale of 1 to 7. At the end of the experiment, you will be asked a few questions about your demographic and language background. This study will take approximately 20-30 minutes to complete.

Benefits
